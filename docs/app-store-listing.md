## App Store Description (4000 characters)

Scan any barcode. Know exactly what's in it. Revelio gives every food, cosmetic, supplement, and household product an instant science-backed grade — with real citations, personalized to your health goals. No black box. No guessing.

**THE SMARTER WAY TO SHOP**
Point your camera at any barcode and get a full ingredient breakdown in seconds. Revelio cross-references thousands of ingredients against peer-reviewed research — then explains every flag in plain English. Finally, a label reader that doesn't require a chemistry degree.

**SCIENCE BEHIND EVERY SCORE**
Most food apps hand you a letter grade with zero explanation. Revelio shows its work. Every flagged ingredient links to the actual study behind the concern — whether it's a 2023 NIH report on seed oil oxidation, an EWG classification for a cosmetic preservative, or FDA safety thresholds for common food additives. You see the evidence. You make the call.

**PERSONALIZED TO YOU**
Not everyone is avoiding the same thing. Set your health priorities — eliminating seed oils, going gluten-free, managing sodium, cutting synthetic dyes, reducing heavy metals — and Revelio re-weights every product score around what actually matters to you. The same granola bar might score an A for one person and a D for someone avoiding inflammatory oils. Your score is yours.

**ALL 4 SCAN CATEGORIES**

🍎 **Food & Beverages** — Groceries, packaged snacks, beverages, condiments. Flags seed oils (canola, soybean, sunflower), synthetic dyes (Red 40, Yellow 5), high-fructose corn syrup, ultra-processed additives, and 200+ flagged ingredients with severity ratings.

💄 **Cosmetics & Personal Care** — Shampoo, lotion, sunscreen, makeup. Detects endocrine disruptors (parabens, phthalates), carcinogenic preservatives (formaldehyde-releasers), and rates products against EWG's Skin Deep database standards.

💊 **Supplements** — Vitamins, protein powders, pre-workouts. Checks for proprietary blends hiding underdosed ingredients, synthetic binders, heavy metal contamination risk, and bioavailability of claimed nutrients.

🏠 **Home & Cleaning Products** — Dish soap, laundry detergent, air fresheners. Surfaces VOCs, hormone-disrupting surfactants, and ingredients with poor aquatic toxicity profiles — because what you clean with matters too.

**ALLERGEN & SENSITIVITY DETECTION**
Building for a family with mixed dietary needs? Revelio detects the Big 9 allergens (gluten, dairy, nuts, soy, shellfish, fish, eggs, sesame, wheat) and flags hidden sensitivities. Create individual household profiles so every family member gets the right alerts.

**FIND BETTER OPTIONS INSTANTLY**
Red score? Revelio surfaces A-rated alternatives you can actually buy — with side-by-side comparisons so you can make the switch in one tap. Because knowing what's bad is only half the job.

**PANTRY TRACKER**
Scan your whole pantry and get a household health score. See which products are dragging your family's grade down — and what to replace them with. Over time, watch your household score improve as you make smarter swaps.

**SHARE YOUR SCANS**
Spotted a sketchy ingredient? Share a TikTok-ready score card directly from any scan. One tap generates a branded card showing the product name, grade, and top flags — perfect for the "what's actually in your groceries" content that's dominating wellness feeds.

**WHY REVELIO?**
The average American reads 0% of ingredient labels before buying. Revelio changes that — not by lecturing you, but by making the information instant, visual, and impossible to ignore. We built the app we wished existed when we started reading labels ourselves.

Download Revelio. Start scanning. See what you've been missing.
