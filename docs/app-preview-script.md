# Revelio — App Preview Video Script (30 seconds)

**Format:** App Store Preview Video  
**Duration:** 30 seconds  
**Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (portrait, iPhone)  
**Resolution:** 1080 × 1920 minimum (match device screenshot resolution)  
**Audio:** Background music + optional VO (no VO version also needed — many users watch muted)

---

## Storyboard

### [0:00 – 0:04] HOOK — The Problem
**Visual:** Close-up of a real product label in a grocery store — a wall of tiny unreadable ingredient text. Hand holding phone approaches.  
**On-screen text:** *"What's actually in this?"*  
**Music:** Builds in, subtle tension.  
**Cut to:** Revelio app opens — dark UI, clean logo.

---

### [0:04 – 0:09] SCAN — Core Action
**Visual:** Live camera view in the app. User aims at barcode on a bag of chips. Scan reticle locks on. Haptic feedback implied by slight phone vibration animation.  
**On-screen text:** *"Scan any barcode."*  
**Sound:** Soft "lock" sound effect as barcode is detected.  
**Cut to:** Loading spinner for ~0.5s (real feel).

---

### [0:09 – 0:14] RESULT — The Grade
**Visual:** ScanResultCard slides up from bottom. Product image, name, brand. GradeBadge animates in — ring sweeps to show score "D / 34." Red flag chips appear: "Seed Oil ⚠️", "Artificial Dye 🔴", "Preservative ⚠️".  
**On-screen text:** *"Get the real score."*  
**Music:** Slight tension on the "D" reveal.

---

### [0:14 – 0:19] DEPTH — Cited Science
**Visual:** User taps a flag. IngredientExplainerSheet slides up showing: ingredient name, explanation in plain English, citation link ("Source: PubMed"). Scrolls slightly to show detail.  
**On-screen text:** *"Science behind every flag."*  
**Music:** Confident, resolving tone — this is the differentiator moment.

---

### [0:19 – 0:24] SOLUTION — Alternatives
**Visual:** Alternatives section animates in. Two A-rated product cards shown side by side. "Better Choice" badge visible. Subtle "Buy" button highlight.  
**On-screen text:** *"Find better options instantly."*  
**Music:** Upbeat lift.

---

### [0:24 – 0:28] PERSONALIZATION — Family Profiles
**Visual:** Quick montage: Profile tab showing two family members ("Mom", "Emma"). Switch between them — scores change for the same product. Pantry view showing household health score.  
**On-screen text:** *"Personalized for your whole family."*

---

### [0:28 – 0:30] CALL TO ACTION
**Visual:** App icon centered on dark background. Logo animates in: "REVELIO". Tagline fades in below.  
**On-screen text:** *"Know what's really in it."*  
**Sub-text:** *"Download free on the App Store."*  
**Music:** Resolves confidently.

---

## Production Notes

### Version A — With Voiceover
- Tone: calm, confident, slightly clinical. Think: knowledgeable friend, not ad narrator.
- Sample VO for 0:09-0:14: *"Revelio breaks down every ingredient — with grades backed by real science."*

### Version B — Text-Only (muted-friendly)
- All beats communicated via on-screen text overlays
- Required for most App Store auto-play views (muted by default)

### Music Direction
- Genre: Minimal electronic / modern wellness
- No copyright — use Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or Pixabay Music
- Key: Something that builds without being aggressive
- Reference: Calm app promo music energy but slightly more assertive

### Text Overlay Style
- Font: SF Pro Rounded (matches iOS system feel)
- Color: White or `#00FF94` (Revelio accent green) on dark background
- Animation: Fade in at cut point, fade out before next cut

### Capture Method
- Record in Simulator or on physical device using QuickTime screen recording
- Use an iPhone 15 Pro or 16 Pro device frame overlay
- Export at 1080 × 1920 minimum, H.264, under 500MB
