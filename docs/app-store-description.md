## Full Description

Scan any barcode. Know exactly what's in it. Revelio gives every food, cosmetic, and supplement an instant science-backed grade — with real citations, personalized to your health goals. No black box. No guessing.

**THE SMARTER WAY TO SHOP**
Point your camera at any barcode and get a full ingredient breakdown in seconds. Revelio analyzes thousands of ingredients against peer-reviewed research — then explains every flag in plain English.

**SCIENCE BEHIND EVERY SCORE**
Unlike other food apps that hand you a rating with zero explanation, Revelio shows its work. Every flagged ingredient links to the actual study. You decide what to trust.

**PERSONALIZED TO YOU**
Set your health priorities — avoiding seed oils, going gluten-free, managing sodium, cutting additives — and Revelio re-weights every product score around what matters to you. The same cereal might be a B+ for one person and a D for someone with different goals.

**ALLERGEN & SENSITIVITY DETECTION**
Building for a family with mixed dietary needs? Revelio detects common allergens (gluten, dairy, nuts, soy, shellfish, eggs) and flags sensitivities so nothing slips through. Create individual profiles for every household member.

**FIND BETTER OPTIONS INSTANTLY**
Red score? Revelio surfaces A-rated alternatives you can actually buy — with side-by-side comparisons so you can make the switch in one tap.

**PANTRY TRACKER**
Scan your whole pantry and get a household health score. See exactly which products are dragging your family's grade down — and what to replace them with.

**COVERS MORE THAN FOOD**
- 🍎 Food & Beverages
- 💄 Cosmetics & Personal Care  
- 🧴 Cleaning Products
- 💊 Supplements

**KEY FEATURES**
• Instant barcode scanning — EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-E, QR codes
• Letter grades (A–F) with 0–100 score
• Full ingredient-by-ingredient breakdown
• Science citations for every flag
• Personalized scoring based on your health priorities
• Allergen detection across 9 major allergens
• Family profiles — separate settings per household member
• Pantry tracker with household health score
• Cleaner alternative finder
• Scan history with trend tracking
• Shareable score cards for social media

**FREE vs PRO**
Free: 10 scans/day, food products, basic grades
Pro ($4.99/mo or $34.99/yr): Unlimited scans, all 4 categories, full flag explanations, science citations, personalized scoring, pantry tracker, pro alternatives

**WHO IS REVELIO FOR?**
— Health-conscious shoppers who want to know what they're actually eating
— Parents managing kids' diets and household health
— People with food allergies or intolerances who need reliable flagging
— Anyone tired of ingredient labels that read like a chemistry exam
— Wellness enthusiasts tracking what goes into their body

Stop guessing. Start knowing. Download Revelio — your personal ingredient analyst.
