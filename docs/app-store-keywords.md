---

## Keyword Research & Rationale

### Tier 1 — High Intent, High Volume
| Keyword | Why |
|---------|-----|
| `food scanner` | Primary use case, high volume, exact match to core feature |
| `barcode scanner` | Generic but massive search volume; qualifies intent with "food" in name |
| `ingredient checker` | Intent-specific — people searching this want exactly what Revelio does |
| `allergen detector` | High intent from allergy/sensitivity sufferers; low competition vs generic food |
| `food safety` | Broad but relevant; captures health-conscious shoppers at top of funnel |

### Tier 2 — Competitor / Category Terms
| Keyword | Why |
|---------|-----|
| `yuka` | Yuka is the direct competitor; people searching for Yuka are exactly our target user |
| `clean eating` | Lifestyle keyword with strong overlap; captures health-conscious audience |

### Keywords NOT included (already in app name/subtitle — would waste characters)
- "revelio" (app name)
- "health" (too broad, low conversion)
- "nutrition" (covered by food safety/ingredient checker intent)
- "scan" (redundant with scanner)

### Alternatives Considered
- `additive detector` — lower volume than allergen detector
- `food label reader` — good but too long given character limit
- `healthy food app` — too broad, low conversion
- `ingredient analysis` — good but less search volume than checker
- `non toxic` — trending but inconsistent search behavior
